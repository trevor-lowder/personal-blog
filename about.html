About Me

My name is Trevor, and I am a US Air Force Veteran with a passion for tech, learning, and creativity. Looking for a remote job in the wake of the pandemic led me to web development. I quickly fell in love with the thrill of learning programming languages, which paralleled my experience learning Japanese when I was stationed in Japan during my time in the military, and solving complex problems, like the challenging puzzles in RPGs that I enjoy. Transitioning into tech has been a challegning and rewarding journey, and I'm very excited to see where it takes me in the future. Thanks for visiting, and if you want to learn more and connect with me, check out my GitHub and LinkedIn.
